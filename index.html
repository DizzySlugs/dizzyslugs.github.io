About

Tornado Tower is a VR third person side-scrolling platformer made in Unreal Engine 4 for the Oculus Touch. Follow the adventure of a young prince as he explores the Tornado Tower with the aid of a whimisical wind spirit to look for the person responsible for the death of his family.

Anton Starodub

Creative Director


Anton was born in picturesque town Khmelnitskiy in central Ukraine. He received his bachelor’s degree in Automation at National Aviation University in Kiev. He has experience in AI, cryptography, and embedded systems. For him, the most beautiful thing about video games is that they combine all the kinds of artistic expressions. Since he likes traveling, music, literature, and visual arts, games are a perfect fit for him as a field for personal expression. He does not believe he can partake into a business he is not passionate about. Guess what, he love games.
